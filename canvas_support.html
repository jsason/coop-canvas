Panopto in SFU Canvas Support

For SFU Beedie Panopto specific questions, please contact your Panopto Admin team (diliara_nasirova@sfu.ca for ExecED & undergrad instructors / crstech@sfu.ca for Grad Program instructors).
The following document includes step by step instructions on working with Panopto Videos in SFU Canvas.

How to list Panopto Video in SFU Canvas Modules

You can list Panopto Video in Canvas Modules in two steps:

1.1 Go to your class Canvas site and then click on Panopto Video in the course menu.

1.2 Choose an existing video recording; click on Share

1.3 A pop-up window will appear; Click on the Copy Link button. Close the pop-up window.

1.4 Navigate to the Modules and click on the plus icon.
