Frequently Asked Questions

For SFU Beedie Panopto specific questions, please contact your Panopto Admin team:

diliara_nasirova@sfu.ca for ExecED & undergrad instructors.

crstech@sfu.ca for Grad Program instructors.


If you encounter the following “Sign in to Panopto” screen, please make sure to click on Panopto Video, which will sign you in Panopto Video automatically. If Panopto Video is not available or this issue persists, please contact your instructor and they will connect you to the Panopto admin team.

If you encounter the following error message: “We were unable to sign you in because your browser is not accepting cookies”. Please enable third-party cookies in your browser settings or use a different browser. If this issue persists, please contact your instructor and they will connect you to the Panopto admin team.

Students can upload videos to the Panopto Assignment folder on their mobile devices. Make sure you temporarily enable “Allow Cross-Website Tracking” to enable cookies for Panopto to work in your mobile browser.
